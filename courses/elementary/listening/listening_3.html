LISTENING

1 Listen to the interview with Mr Smith about his lifestyle. Put a tick (✔) if the information is correct. Put a cross (X) if the information is incorrect.

1 Mr Smith gets up at 6.30 a.m. 

2 Mr Smith goes to work by train. 

3 Mr Smith has lunch in a restaurant. 

4 Mr Smith usually finishes work at 7.30 p.m. 

5 Mr Smith often goes out in the evening. 

2 Listen to five conversations. Put a tick (✔) the correct answers.

1 What does Dan want to drink? 

2 What does Sue do before she goes to bed?

3 How often does Karen go to the gym?

4 What month is Jason’s birthday?

5 What time does Polly get up during the week?
